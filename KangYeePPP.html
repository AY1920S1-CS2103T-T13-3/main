Lim Kang Yee – Project Portfolio for treasurerPro (tP)

This project portfolio briefly introduces the project, treasurerPro and outlines my contributions to the project and explains the feature I implemented.

1. About the team

My team consists of 5 software engineering students under the module CS2103T. 4 of us, including me, are year 2 Computer Science students and the other is a year 4 Computer Engineering student.

2. About the project

This project is part of the module Software Engineering Project, CS2103T where we were tasked to develop a basic command line interface desktop application by morphing or enhancing an existing AddressBook desktop application. Our team decided to incorporate and morph the AddressBook application as part of our all-in-one application which enables treasurers or members of Co-Curricular Activities (CCA) Clubs and Societies to manage their club finances, reimbursements to members, inventory and member’s contact details easily.
